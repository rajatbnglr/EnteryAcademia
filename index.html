Welcome to Entri Academia

Entri is a free learning app that offers video classes and tests for job exams. This app provides study materials in various languages, including Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada. The daily practice app includes mock tests, previous papers, and GK current affairs. Expertly crafted topic-wise quizzes are updated daily. You can experience high-quality video classes and download them for offline learning. The app also offers test series based on Quant, Reasoning, and English. You can attempt hand-picked mock tests available on the app. There is a 24/7 discussion platform to interact with experienced faculty and toppers. You can get daily news on the latest exam notifications. The app offers a smart AI-enabled and detailed analysis to identify weak areas. Entri provides courses for Python, Data Science and ML, Web and Android development, Digital Marketing, Stock market, and more.
